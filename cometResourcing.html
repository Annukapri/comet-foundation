Project Resourcing with COMET

We are introducing COMET Resource augmentation process With a noble approach to assist a greater number of underprivileged and talented students from remote places, in turning their potential skills into success stories.

Along with the needy students, this approach will also benefit the aiding organizations in multiple ways (as explained below).



COMET Resourcing Model


  1. The process starts with an organization who is interested to adopt this resourcing strategy with COMET Foundation

  2. The partnering organizations provide COMET students (B.Tech, BCA, B.Sc IT students) some training and high-quality assignments. This training is related to the organization’s line of business and it is one time activity from the organization side. Once the first batch of COMET students is trained, the subsequent training to the new batches will be conducted by COMET students internally.

  3. COMET students complete the assignments and delve deeper into the subject to improve their abilities. under the guidance of COMET mentors/helping hearts.

  4. When the aiding organizations need project resources, they can contact COMET and get the trained students for their projects. The lead time to fulfill a resourcing request from the COMET side can be 2-8 weeks.

  5. With this resourcing model, the aiding organization can hire skilled interns for FREE. However, the company can pay a certain sum (in accordance with industry standards) to the COMET Foundation when hiring experienced applicants from COMET or when turning the interns to full-time employees.

Contact us if you would like to consider this resourcing model for your organization.



Benefits to the aiding organization

This resource model offers the following benefits to the organization –

  • Opportunity to the organization to be a part of great social initiative, with a good benefit in return.

  • availability of skilled resources for business on schedule and at no cost

  • If the organization is interested, it can use the COMET center to house the work of the COMET-selected students, which will save money on infrastructure requirements..

  • If the organization is interested, it can sign MOU/agreement with COMET management team and outsource the work to the COMET .

  • If organization sponsors with paid certification, CSR funding or donation, they can leverage tax exemption under section 80g.
